It’s the return of dream teams with Dilwale — Shah Rukh Khan, Kajol and Rohit Shetty, joined by Pritam, Varun Dhawan and Kriti Sanon. The musical journey begins with the romantic Gerua, gets super-fun with Manma Emotion Jaage and continues with a complete soundtrack that’s packed with heart.
